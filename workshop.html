Game Development Workshops

for female- and nonbinary-identifying people, no experience necessary!

Time: April 23rd 9:30 to 11:00am PST

Fill out this form if you’re interested and we’ll add you to our mailing list for any updates! Also, join our discord here for more updates, info, and to meet new people!


we have three tracks:

Baby Chick

Never programmed before? No problem! We’ll get you started with block-coding in Scratch, and you’ll be creating a simple game in no time!

Fledgling

Want a challenge that’s a step-up from block-coding? We’ll help you create a game in Minecraft.

Mother Hen

Comfortable with programming and want some more control over gameplay and graphics? We’ll get you familiar with creating your own game in Unity.

Additional assistance in Mandarin, Cantonese, and Spanish available!


After the initial workshops, we’ll keep in touch through our community discord help you along your game development journey. Once you’re comfortable with Scratch or Minecraft, we can help you get started with the next step up!
