who are we?

what’s this hen-semble up to?

we’re a team of students trying to change the world, one game at a time. we give female and nonbinary students the tools to advocate for topics they’re passionate about through game dev—by teens for teens.

a safe space

we’ll take you under our wings

the goal is to bridge the gender gap in game dev and computer science by creating an environment for women and gender minorities to learn how to create games for anyone and everyone to play. here at chook studios we want to center their voices and foster a community where they feel safe to undertake the often vulnerable process of learning.

our values

*spoiler alert* we don’t like fowl play

at our core, we’re a team. like the author of Little Women (Louisa May Alcott) says, "it takes two flints to make a fire," and while we aren’t starting any fires, teamwork ignites the best ideas. And remember, ideas aren’t always perfect right out of the box. Accept that messiness is part of the process.

we believe in our hands lies the power to change the world. let’s use our superpowers to make our communities a little bit better, one pixel at a time.

injoy might not be a real word, but it embodies our love for fun (and it fits the scheme so we’re going with it). we might not always know where we’re going, but let’s have fun in the now, let’s be IN JOY.

this might be the only noun of the list, but the verb form just wasn’t doing it. teamwork begins with trust. And trust begins with truth. (sorry not sorry, we love alliteration). The truth isn’t always easy, but trust me, it can make or break the strongest foundation.

take action, take (calculated) risks, go that extra mile. let’s be the next generation of leaders because why not. also, in case you were wondering, there isn’t some super difficult ritual you have to survive to join the team—just fill out this form.
